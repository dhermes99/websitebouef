Boeuf

63 William St S, Dublin 2, D02 CX86

At Bouef, we specialize in top-quality beef sourced from the finest Irish farms. Our menu features premium cuts, including Ribeye and T-Bone, grilled to your preferred level of doneness. Our free-range poultry is expertly seasoned with our signature blend, ensuring a flavorful and juicy experience with every bite. Each dish is prepared with the utmost care, bringing out the best in every ingredient.

Boeuf & Coq

20 Suffolk St, Dublin 2

At Bouef & Coq, we offer a unique fusion of beef and poultry dishes, using only the best locally-sourced ingredients. Our beef is chargrilled to perfection, while our free-range chicken is seasoned with our exclusive spices for an exceptional taste. Our dishes are crafted to highlight the rich flavors of our high-quality ingredients, served with an array of gourmet sides and sauces that complement each meal.
